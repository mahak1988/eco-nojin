# Press Release: Global Launch of Econojin Platform

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**Date:** 2026-06-15
**Location:** Tehran, Iran (with simultaneous events in 12 pilot sites)

## Econojin Platform Launches Across 12 Global Pilots

### A Paradigm Shift in Dryland Landscape Management

Today marks a historic milestone in sustainable land management as the Econojin platform officially launches across 12 pilot sites spanning 4 continents and 9 countries. This launch represents the culmination of years of research, community engagement, and technological innovation aimed at addressing the intertwined crises of water, soil, livelihoods, and climate change in drylands.

### Global Reach, Local Impact

The platform is now operational in:

- **Iran (4 pilots):** Dishmok, Behbahan, Rodbar/Talesh, Snow Mountain
- **Morocco:** Ouarzazate
- **Jordan:** Wadi Rum
- **Senegal:** Sahel region
- **Ethiopia:** Highlands
- **India:** Rajasthan
- **Australia:** Outback
- **Chile:** Atacama
- **Mongolia:** Steppe

### A New Paradigm

"Econojin is not just another project—it is a paradigm shift," said the project leadership. "We are moving from fragmented, sectoral approaches to integrated landscape management that simultaneously addresses water security, land degradation, ecosystem restoration, livelihood resilience, carbon sequestration, and participatory governance."

### International Alignment

The platform is fully aligned with:
- UN Sustainable Development Goals (2, 6, 8, 13, 15)
- UNFCCC Nationally Determined Contributions (NDCs)
- UNCCD Land Degradation Neutrality (LDN) targets
- Green Climate Fund (GCF) standards
- World Bank Environmental and Social Framework

### Community at the Center

Unlike traditional top-down approaches, Econojin places local communities at the heart of decision-making. The platform integrates Indigenous knowledge (qanats, pastoral commons, traditional water management) with cutting-edge technology (satellite monitoring, IoT sensors, AI-driven decision support systems, blockchain-verified carbon credits).

### First Real-World Data

Today also marks the first real-world data collection from IoT sensors deployed across all 12 pilot sites, measuring soil moisture, water levels, vegetation health, and weather conditions in real-time.

### First MRV Cycle and PES Payments

Within 90 days, the platform will complete its first Measurement, Reporting, and Verification (MRV) cycle and issue the first Payments for Ecosystem Services (PES) directly to community members via blockchain-based smart contracts.

### About Econojin

Econojin (Hydroma Nozhin) is a 10-year integrated landscape management program that combines Climate-Smart Agriculture (CSA), Integrated Water Resources Management (IWRM), Sustainable Land Management (SLM), Land Degradation Neutrality (LDN), and Nature-based Solutions (NbS) with participatory governance and cutting-edge technology.

### Cost-Effective and Scalable

With an average intervention cost of less than $1,000 per hectare, Econojin demonstrates that sustainable landscape management can be both effective and affordable, making it scalable across the MENA region and beyond.
